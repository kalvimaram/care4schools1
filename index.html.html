Our Story

From a Single Tweet
to a Movement.

In 2022, a single tweet about crumbling school toilets in rural Tamil Nadu sparked something unexpected. What began as one person's outrage quickly grew into a collective mission — and Kalvi Maram Foundation's "Care4Schools" project was born.

Safe Drinking Water

We have installed and maintain RO water purifiers across 52 government schools, providing 6,900+ students with consistent, daily access to safe, clean drinking water.

Clean Sanitation

We provide weekly deep toilet cleaning support to selected rural government schools — ensuring hygienic, dignified facilities that keep children, especially girls, in school.

"We believe every child deserves clean water, safe sanitation, quality guidance, and the chance to dream big."

— Kalvi Maram Foundation
